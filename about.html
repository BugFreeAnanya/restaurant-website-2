About Osheen Cafe and Restaurant

Osheen Cafe & Restaurant in Hoshangabad is a popular cafe and fast-food spot in the Rasuliya area, known for its cozy ambiance, coffee, and vegetarian food. It is often described as one of the oldest restaurants in the area and is noted for having a well-designed interior with warm lighting. The cafe offers home delivery, accepts credit and debit cards, and has free street parking.
